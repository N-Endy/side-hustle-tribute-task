Dr. Nnamdi Azikiwe, First Executive President of Nigeria. He was one of the pioneers for independence in Nigeria

Here is a timeline of Dr. Azikiwe's life

  • 1906 - Born in Zungeru, Nigeria.
  • 1925 - Arrived in the United States.
  • 1934 - Founded a national newspaper in then Gold Coast (now Ghana).
  • 1948 - Was elected into the Nigerian Legislative Council.
  • 1954 - Served as Premier to the eastern region of Nigeria.
  • 1972 - Councillor to the University of Lagos.
  • 1996 - Died and was buried in Onitsha

Below are facts you may not know about Dr. Azikiwe's life

  1. Dr. nnamdi Azikiwe held the traditional title of Owelie of Onitsha
  2. He could speak Hausa, Igbo and Yoruba fluently
  3. He was once sentenced to imprisonment
  4. He contested for presidency twice and failed
